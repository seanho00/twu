Corrections

 By popular demand, I am accepting corrections/make-up work on HW5-10: re-submit questions that you got wrong on any of the homeworks HW5-10, and half of those marks will be added to your homework.
E.g., you missed one question worth 10pts on HW6, so you re-do that question and submit it. The re-done work still has some errors, so it's worth 8/10. The net result is you get 4pts added to your HW6 grade.
Please submit the re-done questions on a separate paper (or electronically) to me before the start of our final exam, 22Apr 9am. Please write neatly and show all your work clearly.
