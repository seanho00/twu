  1. Compare and contrast an alias, shallow copy, and deep copy. [6]
    Aliases are two names that refer to the same object.
    Shallow copies are two distinct objects that have attributes/elements that are aliases of each other.
    Deep copies are two distinct objects that do not share any aliases of attributes/elements, no matter how deep in the tree.
