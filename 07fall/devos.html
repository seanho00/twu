Class Devotionals: Fall 2007

One of the wonderful joys and freedoms at a Christian university is being able to start off each class with a devotional and a short prayer! I usually just choose a passage from my own personal devotions that week. The reason why we study and work is to be good stewards of the gifts God gave us, to use them to serve others to bring glory to the Creator who gave them to us. ``To whom more is given, more is required.'' (Luke 12:48)
